Sorcerer boxes may yet update thanks to Bent Jakobsen's source repository.
It may not always be up or always be current.
But it may help the more forgotten Sorcerer boxes get back on the rails.
Ideally, we want sorcerer.aakin.net to point to sorcerer.silverice.org,
but that may or may not happen.
However, if you want to get back on the rails you can also modify
/root/.sorcery/grimoire/grimoire and adjust the URL.

# augur merge
# sed  -i  "s/aakin.net/silverice.org/"  /root/.sorcery/grimoire/grimoire

The above will work if one wants to modify many boxes via ssh.
However, boxes that automatically weekly update may re-rail
themselves from the grimoire available in Bent Jakobsen's source repository.

While the rolled grimoires are full of updates the PPR is still offline.
aakin.net and silverice.org are considerably different and the PPR
is considerably complex.
Perhaps the PPR will be working by the first week of December?
You may want to hold off the linux update, for example, until
the PPR is available again.

Those of you who have had Sorcerer deployed since 2000 know
that about once every year or two Sorcerer migrates.
Historically:

kublai.com -> ibiblio.org -> savannah.org -> berlios.de ->
aakin.net -> silverice.org

Sorcerer's life expectancy is 2010 and
therefore will probably move
yet again before being retired.
Of course it keeps getting better and therefore
you may want to stick around while the ride lasts.
