# mkdir -p /usr/share/sorcery/local
# chmod 0755 /tmp/$SPELL
# mv /tmp/$SPELL /usr/share/sorcery/local/

There is also another location that can be used,
unofficial for SAs that do not want to keep custom
stuff somewhere under /usr/local
One could place spells in:
/usr/local/share/sorcery/local/

However, I strongly suggest placing custom spells
in /usr/share/sorcery/local/

For optimal merge speed the local grimoire
can also be /usr/share/sorcery/local.tar
If one has more than 64 local spells
then a tarball can speed up merging
especially when those files are
not already in the kernel cache.

/var/lib/sorcery is not used by sorcery anymore.
Assuming nothing is symbolic linked in /etc/sorcery
to /var/lib/sorcery the it should be safe to remove
/var/lib/sorcery
