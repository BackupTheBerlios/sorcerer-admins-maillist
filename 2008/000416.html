Many years have elapsed since I previously used initramfs images to boot.
Consequently, I forgot an important lilo global parameter.
It is the "compact" parameter.
Consequently, the initramfs and kernel images are loaded faster.

Before "compact" I saw so many dots while lilo was loading
that I expected pacman to appear.
But with the "compact" lilo parameter
the kernel and initramfs load in a second.
Much better.
