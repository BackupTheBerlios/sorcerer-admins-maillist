By now all boxes should have dual column ELF logs
located entirely in /etc/sorcery/log/elf/
/etc/sorcery/log/ldd/ should be empty or not exist.
However, I received a recent report that suggests
that some boxes do not have 2 column ELF logs.
Therefore, I re-enabled the elf log fix in sorcery.
I suppose that I will probably stay enabled for the rest of 2008.
When a transition happens in Sorcerer the code
to bridge the transition remains active between 3 and 6 months.
Please update boxes no less frequently than quarterly.
