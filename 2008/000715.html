Kyle Sallee wrote:
> I recommend:
>
> # rm /etc/sorcery/log/*/grimoire-failed
> # cast grimoire
>
> Failed spells are used to clean up files from a spell
> that encountered after prepare_install and before successfully casting.
> *-failed spells are always removed by cast.
> However, if for some odd reason one survived
> it can normally be dispelled.
> However, in this case do not dispel it, just remove the log files.
> A new version of the grimoire always overwrites the old version.
> So dispelling it might leave the box without an installed grimoire,
> if the grimoire spell is not concurrently installed.
>
> A long time ago Sorcerer SAs requested that if a spell should
> fail after prepare_install and before success
> then the files installed should be tracked,
> removed, and that the previously installed spell should
> be restored as installed.
> Therefore, that is what cast tries to do.
>
