I have had success with just the default rules and sound.rules installed.
However, I use linux-modular along with modular sound modules.
Maybe as Jan suggests additional rules may be required if the sound
card drivers are not loaded as modules?
After alsa is re-installed please issues:

# /etc/init.d/udevd stop; /etc/init.d/udevd start; alsactl trigger

That will probably create device nodes with proper permission.
If the problem still exists try recasting alsa-lib

Rebooting should not be required.
