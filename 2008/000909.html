Hi,

I'm sending this e-mail to let you know that I have just brought a new
source cache server online.

The new server is on my end of a 20 MB down/1 MB up fixed line. It is
powered by a low power Atom processor (and further more in a virtual
machine), therefore if you get a poor performance then you know why :).

The reason for building a new machine was that the old hardware was
unstable, and used to much power (around 600 W against the current 17 W).

I have leeched the most sources, but most likely forgotten some.  I'll hope
very soon to investigate which ones I have forgotten :)

Regarding the availability of the sources. I want to have the right, to
every now and then, nuke the sources on my server (par example if I suspect
that one or more files gets corrupted or I just want to releech them again
or what ever might happen in the future).

If you experience any problems with the new source cache server, then do
please drop me a line.
