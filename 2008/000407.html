An I/R from 2006 is totally useless on updated Sorcerer boxes.
When booted from an old I/R one can not chroot into the fixed disk filesystem.
Programs on the fixed disk filesystem will not run.
It will instead print: "The kernel is too old."

This probably has something to do with the way glibc is compiled.
The oldest kernel supported by glibc 2.7 as compiled by the spell is
probably 2.6.19.
Removing support for older kernels
increase glibc performance and reduce glibc size.
But this change also makes older I/Rs obsolete
for boxes that are updated.

Therefore, I advise everyone to download 20080301 I/Rs,
before a time comes when an I/R may be needed to rescue a box.
