Old style spells will be indexed properly if the "legacy" feature is enabled.
Otherwise, only new style spells will index properly.
Indexing was optimized again.
Merely a few percents were gained during this optimization.

That which effects indexing most are spells.
Inheriting large spells slows indexing.
Large spells by themselves slow indexing.
Spells like vim and jumpnbump,
which have an enormous amount of sources,
require an enormous amount of miliseconds to index.
While spells are indexed as bash executable scripts
indexing speed is nearing maximum.
I hope that indexing is sufficiently fast on everyone's boxes.
