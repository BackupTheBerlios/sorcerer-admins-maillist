Fear not!
2009 has not yet arrived.
However, I moved the 2009 Sorcerer I/R design
to the front of my todo list.
And it is ready.

Unfortunately, the 2009 design sufficiently differs from the 2008 design
that sdelta3 update patches from the 2008 to the 2009 design are not possible
However, sdelta3 patches will be available for 2009 design updates.

With the compact 2009 design about 55% of available disk space remains.
Therefore, future I/Rs have plenty of room for expansion
while still being burnable to a CD.
What do you want to have on the next Sorcerer I/R release?

As usual I tried to squish the bugs.
A complete install and post install boot test
was performed successfully on the IA32 I/R
But a perfect I/R is a rare event.
Please report bugs and fixes to the email list.
