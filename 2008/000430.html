The above is a the format that I decided upon for the new
log /etc/sorcery/log/dispel.gz
This way the stored information requires less disk space
while providing potentially useful information
that is easy to grep and sed.

However, be aware that a 4th and more
tab separated columns may be available
when dispel was invoked with more than one
spell on the command line or in the dispel queue.
For the reason of raw speed dispel does not
run on a per spell basis which is why there will
not be /etc/sorcery/log/dispel/$SPELL
individual per spell dispel logs.

This additional functionality comes
for no additional cost in speed.
This version of dispel is faster than
what boxes currently use.
