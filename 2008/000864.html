I suspect that X11 takes the keyboard configuration from HAL and your 
xorg.conf also tries to configure the same keyboard. Also the options asking 
for pc104 layout may be invalid for evdev contolled devices, not sure about 
that.

Without knowing more details about your setup, I cannot give you a better 
answer. 

It would be helpfull for me to see the /etc/X11/xorg.conf, /var/log/Xorg.0.log 
and output from lshal. Alternatively, send at least the following output:

 grep -C2 -i -e xkb -e kbd -e keyb -e evdev /var/log/Xorg.0.log

 sections ServerLayout, InputDevice (keyboards only), ServerFlags from 
xorg.conf

 lshal | grep -C15 -i keyb

Thanks, 

Jan
