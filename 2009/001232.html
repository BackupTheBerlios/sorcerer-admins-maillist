> after booting into the system when you sit on the login screen . How

All output from sysinit is directed to a non console tty.
All output from the runlevel is directed to a different non console tty.
If anything still scrolls on console
then it probably wrote to console
or wrote at a loglevel that permits
the kernel to write the the message to console and/or all active ttys
