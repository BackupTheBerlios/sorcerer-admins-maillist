Hi, back from my winter vacation

I was happy to read that my "flame" did have a positive feedback on my 
problems. 

Just thinking why we actually need this whole compiler menu in first 
place? could we just copy needed environment from I/R? We have 
linux-modular, no need to compile custom kernel anymore. Let's just copy 
needed stuff to new root and SA then can reboot and use sorcerer tools on 
a new installed environment. 

Regarding X.org. Whole force behind Sorcerer was earlier that you could 
choose your depencies, but now this X.org is sort of forced to install. So 
whats the point being source based if we still force big spells, eg. X.org 
to be force installed and removing it and it's depencies so damn 
difficult? I simply dont like it, we are loosing our freedom here on big 
way.
Whats next, whole GNOME or KDE is forced too?

I dont mind that it exists on I/R, but at least there should be options to 
be "server option" where X.org should not follow nor its depencies. I 
agree that nowdays some server programs do use GUI stuff and some has some 
X Window library depencies but there should be way to simpy cast that 
needed library and it's depencies not bunch of multimedia crap.
