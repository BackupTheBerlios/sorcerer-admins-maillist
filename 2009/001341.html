That sounds as if someone discovered
the hard way that the newer xorg servers
are not compatible with currently released xorg video drivers.
Whether or not re-compiling an xorg video driver
will solve that, I do not know.

Having discovered this with my own testing
many months ago I progressed as far as
possible using the available xorg source tarballs.

My guesses are that:
either the currently selected stable xorg tarballs
should be re-tested and made into a compatible set;
or the upgrade method must force re-compiling
of xorg video drivers if that might be a solution.
However, the second guess might not solve the problem.
Most video drivers probably will not recompile.
