Great question.
There are two easy methods.
Proxy support can be controlled by environment variables
or by modifying /root/.curlrc.sorcery

The information for both methods is in the curl manual page.
$ man curl

Since I have not used a proxy in over a decade,
except when playing with ziproxy and junk buster,
I recommend asking on the curl email list it fails to work.
