/etc/gconf/gconf.xml.defaults/

/usr/share/glib-2.0/schemas/
are used directly or processed into something else.

the commented out instructions at the bottom of the GConf spell
that will regenerate the files /etc/gconf/gconf.xml.defaults/
from the files in /etc/gconf/schemas/
