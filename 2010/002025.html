For those looking for something fun to do
FlightGear can be compiled and run again.
I discovered how to avoid the problem
that occurred while compiling OpenSceneGraph
which is a requirement for FlightGear.
I enjoy playing FlightGear a few times during December.
And for those who want to enjoy some virtual sledding
there is the extremetuxracer spell.

Sorcerer does not have a huge selection of games,
but there are some like penguin-command
that help SAs from becoming too overworked.
It is like 1980s Atari "Missile Command" except
it has cool mod music and booming explosion sound effects.  :)

Try not to get too stressed out at the end of the year.
penguin-command does not require OpenGL and
therefore can be played on any computer.
My best high score is over 300K, good luck beating 100K.
