For what it might be worth:

# augur grimoire | grep "Yore\|Fore" | wc -l
0

The selection of words such as Yore and Fore
provide an unusually good anchor words for sedding and grepping
the output of # augur event.

Much consideration was given to the selection of words,
the order of the columns, the information provided,
and the way event information is stored in the file system.
More than 10 revisions happened before the implementation
satisfactorily meet the expectation.
Although the change might appear to be simple and done on a whim,
somewhere between 1 and 2 weeks worth of effort went into the new events system.
2 weeks is a very light commitment for
the completion of a Sorcerer sub-project.
And if not for Evert's request for changes in augur scry
then I might have left the old event system in place for
yet another few years.

Contrasting the appearance and information
with the old event system gives reason to expect
that any aspect of Sorcerer has potential for improvement
provided that a Sorcerer SA allocates
the required time and effort to make it happen.
