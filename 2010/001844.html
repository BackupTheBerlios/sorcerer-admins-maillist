Maybe I forgot to do something?

Try:
# augur forget tcp_wrappers
# augur merge
# cast openssh

It should work.
I have boxes with openssh installed,
but not tcp_wrappers installed.

# augur version tcp_wrappers; augur version openssh
tcp_wrappers inactive
5.5p1

Alternatively, you could try rerolling a tcp_wrappers
tarball and casting it.
The tcp_wrappers spell works provided that
content from the tcp_wrappers tarball can be extracted
without error.
