Congratulations....

Seems that you are over the biggest hurdle in Sorcerer.

Some commands for the root user:
augur sync
    synchronizes the computer with the latest stuff.

augur newer
   shows you newer versions of what you have installed.

augur update actually updates your software.


However, I have a suspicion that this is not what you are after.

issuing:

cast kde-v4

should leech & compile everything you need for a full fledged K-desktop on
top of X11.

I would not recommend it, though. ( As you noticed, Sorcerer is in heavy
development, and may not work as expected )

What I generally do in your cast, is:

cast xorg-server

Once you have X up and running, you can cast kde-v4, if you are interested
in a K desktop.

Otherwise, you can always type
sorcery
at the command line, and explore the menus....
