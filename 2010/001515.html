Interesting, i commented out from /etc/netconfig these lines:
udp6       tpi_clts      v     inet6    udp     -       -
tcp6       tpi_cots_ord  v     inet6    tcp     -       -

Then restarted rpcbind, after that, rpc.statd did started! Problem solved.
