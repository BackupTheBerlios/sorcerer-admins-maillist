Ah, I was just writing a guide on that.

Once you made the partitions, they have to be formatted. ( And given a
filesystem type, I recommend ext3 )
I believe there is an option for formatting in Sorcerer menu.

If the partition was not formatted, it will not mount.

Don't dispair, please. It IS possible to get Sorcerer installed, and a
highly rewarding experience too. Unfortunately it's a bit of a learning
curve. Stick with it, it's well worth the trouble. ( It took me a week to do
it the first time around, and considered myself quite versed in the ways of
computers back then. ) ( Many years on, I realize I know almost nothing. )
