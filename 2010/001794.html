They might have failed requirements
that are causing those spells to autofail.
Probably the correct way to avoid that is to
# augur clear fail
But I usually type:
# rm -r /root/.sorcery/queue/fail

After that issue:

# cast initramfs linux udev

And they might compile provided
that the software they require is installed
even if the installed software is an older
than current version.


The problems described seem to have the common
stem of gobject-introspection.
Please do the
# cast cairo gobject-introspection
prior to the above fixes to solve the root problem
before attempting to prune the branches.
