/usr/lib/pkgconfig is the standard location.
Both the cmake and configure methods for compilation
export PKG_CONFIG_PATH=/usr/share/pkgconfig
In the past that solved the problem
of a few strange gnome sources installing
pkgconfig files into /usr/share/pkgconfig
instead of /usr/lib/pkgconfig
Oh I see gdm has a custom build()
I added the export to gdm's build.
That aught to fix it.
Thanks for the report.
