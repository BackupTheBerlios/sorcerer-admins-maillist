Hi all

I have just shutdown Celeborn.silverice.org machine, which has been served 
several years Sorcerer stuff. It was a small desktop like machine running 
AMD processor, 1 GB RAM and 200GB HDD. It served our purposes fine but it 
was very old hardware and our needs have been grown out of it, like 
missing 64-bit support, not much memory, etc. 

We have been migrating old stuff to new servers on this year and our 
project has currently using 3 servers for Sorcerer. All virtualised under 
Virtualbox. New systems can use total up to 8 GB RAM, 4 (physical) CPU 
cores and has 1.5TB RAID HDD Space. Net access goes thru 2 different ISP 
fiber cables, also we are getting upgrade to net bandwidth, 100Mbit/sec is 
coming on this summer. Kyle has a full root access to those machines. Also 
i do welcome other developers too to use those machines (if Kyle allows 
;-) ). 

Anyway if you notice that some (web) service is no longer answering please 
inform so we can redirect all non-working traffic to new servers, to new 
addresses.
