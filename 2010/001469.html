Thank you for the bug report.
However, please also report bugs caused by software authors
to the software authors' email lists in order for
those software authors to become aware of their bugs
and fix them upstream.

Just because almost any bug
can be fixed at the distribution level
does not mean that every bug
should be fixed at the distribution level.
gentoo developers decided to implement
both the simple and also INCORRECT solution.
Just because distribution maintainers can
does not mean that they should
assume any responsibility for the fitness of software
which the distribution neither provides
as a source tarball nor a pre-compiled binary package.

P.S.
I am disabling tcp_wrappers support in stunnel.
It compiles fine that way.
I do not care if stunnel authors fix the bug or not.
tcp_wrappers made no new releases since 1997.
It is dead, gone, decayed, and pushing up daisies.
It even requires a 3K patch to compile.
It should not belong on any box that has in-kernel
firewall support such as linux, which has iptables and ipchains.
