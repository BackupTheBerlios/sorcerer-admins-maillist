> Could someone tell me how to erase the current partition windows xp and
> make sorcerer the one and only partition on my OS?

When you get to the part where you have to configure your disk, you'll be
running fdisk or cfdisk.

Simply delete all partitions, then for simplicity's sake, create two
partitions, the first one being your / partition (set it with the bootable
flag), and the second one being your SWAP partition (type 83).

Make your swap partition BIG for Sorcerer, like, a GigaByte. You're going
to need a lot of virtual memory for all the compiling your machine is
going to be performing, and the more actual RAM you have, the better. But
you're still going to need adequate swap space.
