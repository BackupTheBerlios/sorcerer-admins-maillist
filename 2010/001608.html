That box definitely has some troublesome alien files on it.
I recommend a good cleaning.
There should be no /lib/libstdc*
libstdc++* goes into /usr/lib/ or /usr/lib64/

Aliens can cause all sorts of unexpected and difficult
to conjecture problems.
Please cast sorcery-clean
and carefully examine the compile log
and move the problematic aliens to someplace
safe like /alien/lib/ /alien/usr/lib/ /alien/etc/ and so forth...
If everything still works fine a week after
then /alien/ directory can be removed without regret.

I could make sorcery automatically discover
and remove alien files.
But SAs could become annoyed with such
strict enforcement.
Especially, alien files in /etc
are not always unwanted.
So please clean aliens from boxes manually.
