Most boxes receive the LAG=1 grimoire.
It provides a sloppy solution that allows
updating the grimoire on boxes where the SA
sets the date close, but not perfect,
and the box does not know the correct UTC date.

Boxes that use the LAG=1 grimoire
must wait 1 more day before receiving
a current grimoire as compared to the LAG=0 boxes.
Likewise LAG=7 boxes wait 7 days.
In this fashion dev/test boxes can be updated
with a more recent grimoire and production boxes
can use an older grimoire.

To receive the most recently rolled grimoire
set LAG=0 in /etc/sorcery/local.config
# augur merge
# cast grimoire
