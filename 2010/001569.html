Speaking of sorcery...
I am moving almost all the requirements and optionals
off of the sorcery spell and onto the core spell.
If that does not make sense at this time
the effect will become clear when updating using
a grimoire that has the new requirements.
Basically, this change will allow sorcery to update
without first forcing the installtion of gcc-core and gcc-g++.
Also with only the grimoire as a requirement of sorcery
the probability of sorcery being auto-failed is much reduced.

I finished writing a patch to compile cyrus-sasl with the newer gcc.
If anyone detects other sources
that fail compilation with the newer gcc installed
then please report the failures to the list for fixes.
