Sorcerer init-scripts are not LSB compatible init-scripts,
but Sorcerer's is entirely LSB backward compatible.
Therefore, any LSB compatible init-script
provided by a software author can often be
integrated into Sorcerer without modification.
While Sorcerer's average init-script size is about 22 lines
the average size provided by software authors
if many times larger.
But still the backward compatible aspect can
make for useful immediate use of software
without having to read and understand a
huge init-script and rewrite it to what Sorcerer would use.

An init-script execution method that is backward
compatible with LSB and can use LSB style init-scripts
without requiring additional information added to
the comments section is a boon worth keeping.
