ext3 filesystems can be converted into ext4 filesystems.

https://ext4.wiki.kernel.org/index.php/Ext4_Howto#Converting_an_ext3_filesystem_to_ext4
Seems simple from the documentation,
but I have not tried it.
I am keeping my filesystems ext3.
Please make /boot ext2
lilo can load from ext3, but the journal wastes space.
Whether lilo/grub can load from ext4 I have not tested.
It might not work.

So on a box that lacks a dedicated /boot partition
and instead has /boot on /
I do not recommend converting that to ext4.
