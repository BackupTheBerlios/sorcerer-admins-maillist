I just realized I that I have been apologizing
for making a mistake that was not made.
All of the archives on the sorcerer-*-20100207.iso
are xz compressed.
Therefore, the I/Rs come with xz installed.
Therefore, after a box from that I/R is deployed
it should be able to update the installed sorcery and grimoire.
xz should not be a problem on freshly deployed boxes.

When writing recommendations about the latest ISO
please be certain that the ISO9660 image being used
is actually the latest ISO.
Or explicitly state what version of the I/R is being tested.
