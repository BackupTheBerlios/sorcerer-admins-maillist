Odd.
Anyway, copy the library to /var/cache/old/lib/
That should circumvent the problem.
Normally when a spell is dispelled it's entire
installed contents are copied to /var/cache/old/
That way old libraries remain available
and also the spell can be restored
if the version being compiled fails during install.
sorcery should be able to handle this automatically
instead of ending in a situation where
ld.so can not locate a libacl library.
