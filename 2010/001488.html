I use aumix to change levels manually.
And for automatic setting I use /etc/init.d/alsactl
when the box boots.
That requires an /etc/asound.state to work.
First set the levels.
Then, run alsactl store
And that creates the /etc/asound.state file.

Evert tried doing audio with OSSv4 for a while.
Apparently no audio solution is without some quirks.
Fortunately, on my boxes audio seems to work okay.
But at least it is not like in the days where
I had to buy a sound blaster OSS module in order
to have a driver for my sound card.
Overall the support for audio hardware
seems good and getting better.
