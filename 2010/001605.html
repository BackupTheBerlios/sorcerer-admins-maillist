Kyle, thanks for thunderbird, I use it.

For KDE applications in program menu try out to setup XDG_DATA_DIRS and 
PATH. I use xfce and kde applications appear in menu.
In my .bash_profile:

PATH=/opt/kde4/bin:/opt/kde3/bin:$PATH
export 
XDG_DATA_DIRS=/opt/kde4/share/applications/kde4/:/opt/kde3/share/applications/kde/:/opt/kde3/share/:/opt/kde4/share/
