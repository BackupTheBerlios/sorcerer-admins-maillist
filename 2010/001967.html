Hi there...

I do agree with Kyle's rather lengthy mail.

Modifications to init-scripts should not be preserved when an SA casts
init-scripts. This is a sort of fail safe where we can get back to a
known working config when we have been tinkering with bits of the OS
that are quite sensitive.

I do believe that configuration of init-scripts should be handled
similarly to the way we configure networks.
