Prepare to crash into the walls hard and fast.  :)

Fortunately, there is nothing about Sorcerer
that should cause it to destroy hardware.
Accidentally erase partitions, yes, but other
than that it is reasonably safe.  :)
Even the default of not enabling atimes for
ext{2,3,4} filesystems should prolong fixed disk life.
And the compilation of software on tmpfs instead of
a real filesystem also tends to spare disk wear and tear
and avoid unnecessary filesystem fragmentation.
Sorcerer is good to run on hardware for a very long time.
Boxes might actually prefer it.  *lol*
