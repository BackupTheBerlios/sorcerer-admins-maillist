I wholeheartedly agree.

StuntRally refused to release tarballs for their latest version of game...
hence they are now falling behind in versions, and the latest released
tarball does not compile anymore. So, not long from now, it will drop from
the grimoire all together. I asked them nicely to please make this
available, and they just said, no.

There are enough high quality sources out there that want to play ball that
we don't have to cater for the few that feel the need to never release a
tarball.

More on topic... I would love to see a spell for recordmydesktop that
actually works, as it's an optional dependency for Kdenlive.

On 26 July 2012 18:55, Kyle Sallee <kyle.sallee at gmail.com> wrote:

> While visiting the KDE homepage today
> I saw at the top of "Latest Applications"
> an entry for FDesktopRecorder.
> Seems as if desktop recording software
> is leaping out at me now.
> However, after following the URLs
> I discovered no source tarball.
> Therefore, I am not writing a spell for it.
> And so my prejudice is, "no source tarball no spell."
>
> I prefer not to write spells that acquire source trees
> from repositories or from snapshots of repositories.
> That is because the soup du jour
> may be of unexpected quality
> as compared to the source tree
> at a specified release date.
>
> I loathe checking through the source tree at various dates
> looking for the 1 in a 30 that actually compiles.
> I do that for a few spells already.
> And the result tends to be that such spells
> go 6 months to a year without updates.
>
> Now to quote the movie character Dirty Harry,
> "I know what you’re thinking:
> \"Did he fire six shots, or only five?\"
> Well, to tell you the truth,
> in all this excitement,
> I’ve kinda lost track myself.
> But being this is a .44 Magnum,
> the most powerful handgun in the world,
> and would blow your head clean off,
> you’ve got to ask yourself one question:
> \"Do I feel lucky?\" Well do ya, punk?"
>
> Checking several snapshots without
> achieving a successful compilation;
> blows my head clean off.
