The modifications to the spell queues caused some unexpected and
unintentional outcomes.
First, some Sorcerer box might not automatically update.
Second, some boxes might already be looping immune-exam-integrity indefinitely.
Fortunately, the cure to both is sorcery version ist-20120413-0 and
later versions.
These bugs might only effect some "Sorcerer Modern Magic" boxes.
"Sorcerer Sentient" boxes are not effected.
That is because "Sorcerer Sentient" boxes only update when I roll new I/Rs
which I intend to do after I stomp every bug I can find.

Sorry for the inconvenience.
I derail automatic updating about once every 3 to 4 years.
It was probably just about due to happen anyway.
Boxes that are effect should be possible to update by using
the commands in the old fashion way such as:

# au begin grimoire; au scry
Then wait for it to complete.

# au begin sorcery; au scry
Then wait for it also.

# au newer; au queue -immune-exam-integrity; au begin

After sorcery version ist-20120413-0 installs
then boxes can update again using:

# au sync

and automatic updates from immune-auto-sync will function again.

Anyone who uses version progress to manually increase the version of sorcery,
please remember to put it back on stable progress after the fixed
sorcery is installed
otherwise it will freeze forever at that custom version.
