Also I found some interesting make targets for linux
that I did not previously notice.

  localmodconfig  - Update current config disabling modules not loaded
  localyesconfig  - Update current config converting local mods to core

A bit too dangerous to be useful with the linux* spells in the grimoire,
but for those people thinking about transitioning from a modular linux
to a tiny monolithic kernel or just a tinier custom kernel
then one of those make targets might be useful.
Of course then you are on your own for maintaining and updating
your .config if you do so.
