Someone requested desktop capturing/recording software.
Unfortunately, recommendations were not supplied, nor homepages, nor
download URLs.
I normally entirely disregard such requests,
but I was feeling generous.

I managed to find a few potential software projects.
2 of them compile.
I do not know if they run without bugs.
The projects appear to have discontinued
releasing new source tarballs 3,4 or more years ago.
Seems odd that I am writing a spell for obsolete
software that never before appeared in the grimoire.

xvidcap and recordmydesktop could be compiled.
istanbul requires obsolete libraries
that are no longer provided on Sorcerer
and therefore did not compile.
I added a spell for it, with broke and deprecated attributes
just to remind myself that I already examined it.
I will remove the spell sometime in 2013, probably.

Hopefully, this helps fill a potential vacancy in useful software
if gimp nor other available software is unable to do desktop recording.

BTW, I introduced the xvidcap spell several days ago
and recordmydesktop just today.
So expect a few couple of days before those reach
an installed grimoire.
