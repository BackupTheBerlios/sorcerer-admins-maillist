New ia32, x86_64 architecture specific CD images
and a new sources CD image are available for download:

http://sorcerer.silverice.org/download/iso9660/

Please tell if you get the trackerless BitTorrents working.
As usual p7zip compressed sdelta3 update patches are
available for wise and frugal downloaders.

This time please CC me bug reports for the
x86_64 specific CD since I rolled it.
Stepping in for Jan Merka to update the x86_64 CD image
for this time enabled me to identify and circumvent problems
involving the x86_64 architecture specific busybox
which is statically linked with uClibc.
The combination of busybox with uClibc works fine on ia32,
but misbehaves on x86_64.
However, steps to circumvent the problems
appear to make using the combination of uClibc with busybox
on the x86_64 architecture specific initramfs image possible.
This configuration enables the x86_64 architecture specific CD
to scrape the 650M to 700M C writeable limit.

The software available on the ia32 and x86_64
architecture specific CDs are nearly identical.
However, the x86_64 architecture specific CD
is larger due to architecture specific reasons.
