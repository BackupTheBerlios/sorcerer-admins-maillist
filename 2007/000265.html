The /dev that was created during the installation of sorcerer is copied to the
/dev on the real permanent filesystem.
That way if sometime during the boot either the contents of /dev does not exist
or udev fails to start properly
the box might still be able to boot and continue functioning.
Consider it a safety net for udev.
