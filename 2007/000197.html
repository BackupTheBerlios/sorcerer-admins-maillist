Please remember that when you come back from the future
that you must reset both the system clock and hardware clock
on your notebook to the correct UTC time for the present.  ;)

After setting the time and date with the date command, the following command:
# hwclock --utc  --systohc
will save the system clock to the hardware clock.
The hardware clock is battery powered and
is from where the time and date loaded into
the system clock during boot.
Therefore, synchronize both clocks to prevent
the problem from re-occurring again on the next boot.

Lastly if you grow weary of clock drift
then try:
# cast -r ntp
This can help keep time correct on the box by
synchronizing it with other boxes through the Internet.
