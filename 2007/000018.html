Via the provide logs Sorcerer boxes will try to maintain either
an installation of XFree86, or the modular installation of xorg.
The provide logs that state things such as:
libXaw is provided by XFree86
is why libXaw was never cast.
If you want to switch to xorg,
which may be a good idea,
then you should eliminate the
provide logs that state XFree86
should be providing all those libraries and headers and such...

# grep  -rl  XFree86  /etc/sorcery/log/provide |  xargs -l64 rm
# dispel --unsafe XFree86
# cast xorg

After that is installed you will probably no longer have a problem
with missing and/or incorrect symbolic links to libGL libraries.
