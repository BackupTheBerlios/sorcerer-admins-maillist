> I know I keep asking - but isn't there like a ~/.grimoire to put new/custom
> spells in 
You can put something like
LOCAL_GRIMOIRE="/var/lib/sorcery/grimoire.library/local"
into /etc/sorcery/local.config

to define your local grimoire. Of course, you must do augur merge; augur index 
before the changed local spell is available for casting.
