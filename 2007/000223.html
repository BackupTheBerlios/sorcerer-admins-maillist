As a result of the bug that effected md5sums of configuration files
some boxes may have a new ssh key generated for them and as a result
you will have to re-confirm that the new for to the host is valid.
It will say something like

The authenticity of host '....' can't be established.
RSA key fingerprint is .....
Are you sure you want to continue connecting (yes/no)? yes

Simply confirm yes.

This is not necessarily a bad thing for SAs that never bothered
to remove the previous keys for their box after install Sorcerer.
Sorcerer boxes aught to have unique identifying RSA keys.
