On 4/11/07, Jan Merka <merka at highsphere.net> wrote:
> I would suggest using strace and ltrace to find out where exactly Domino
> crashes, i.e. in which library and then try to look where it comes from and
> when it was updated. Maybe this will provide some hints.
>

Ah, that is a great idea...
strace is a good for seeing what system library calls occur in
precompiled binaries
and things that we can not easily trace using gdb, such as ELF library preloads.
Thanks for mentioning it, Jan.

While it may provide a good idea of about where the crash is happening,
unfortunately it may not indicate exactly why it happens.
Even in verbose mode strace can not indicate anything more than
what is passed and returned from system library calls.
But it may provide a clue.
