http://sorcerer.silverice.org/download/iso9660/new_and_testing/

Finally, they are updated.
Did not change anything about the installation or installer.
Probably should have updated that, but I know some of you
are anxious to try out new Install/Rescue disks and new sources.

The IRs are a bit smaller this time,
mostly due to fewer installed version of gcc.

74M sorcerer-ia32-20070905.iso9660.7z
77M sorcerer-x86_64-20070905.iso9660.7z
60M sources-20070905.iso9660.7z

The patches are a bit larger.

41M sorcerer-ia32-20070202-20070905.iso9660.sd3.7z
46M sorcerer-x86_64-20070202-20070905.iso9660.sd3.7z
7.0M sources-20070202-20070905.iso9660.sd3.7z

With the exception of the patches for the source CD
the IR patches are more than half the size of full compressed image.
However, they are still available since on a slow connection the
difference in size could still amount to hours of downloading.

Please report problems even when you can easily circumvent them.
With some luck they should work as good as the previous IRs,
however when it comes to newer versions of busybox and uClibc,
it might not work at all.
