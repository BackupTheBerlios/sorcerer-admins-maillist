It appears to have something to do with the line in
/etc/sorcery/config

export  LESS=${LESS:--FX}

Probably from the less manual page:

       -F or --quit-if-one-screen
              Causes less to automatically exit if the entire file can be dis-
              played on the first screen.

For the moment I will comment out that line.
That way you will be able to switch to less mode for
compile logs that are less than the amount of lines
in your console or terminal emulation.
