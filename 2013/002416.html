Hi there...

My laptop came with GPT.

I ended up using fdisk and putting a normal partition table on it, as it's
drives are not big enough to make GPT an absolute necessity.

However, this is the wave of the future, and the sooner it's integrated
with Sorcery, the better.

-Evert-
P.S. cfdisk does not understand GPT, and this should be mentioned in our
documentation.
