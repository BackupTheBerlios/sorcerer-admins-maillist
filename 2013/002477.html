/etc/sorcery/config becomes /usr/libexec/sorcery/config
/etc/sorcery/local.config becomes /etc/sorcery/config
The change prevents neophytes from accidentally editing
a file that should not be edited.

The transition requires nothing more than a normal update.
This email is to convey awareness and mitigate potential confusion.
