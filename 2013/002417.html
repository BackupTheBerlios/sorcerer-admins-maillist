On 02/18/2013 10:43 AM, Marc Grondin wrote:
> Hello,
> I am interested in trying Sorcerer my only problem is that the installer
> does not see the partitions on my 2TB GPT drive. How can i install sorcerer
> on this drive?

Not a problem. Just use parted or gdisk to format the drive. You can use
lilo too as long as your / isn't bigger than 2TB, IIRC, but who in their
right mind would want to mount / on a partition larger than 2TB anyway?

It's still recommended to have a /boot partition, and the same rules
still apply prohibiting /boot to be on XFS.

I hope that helps :)

Enjoy!
