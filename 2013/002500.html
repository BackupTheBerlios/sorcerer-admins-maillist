> If we want to adjust performance
> then utilization of CPU frequency and governors

Ah, intel processors use P-states now, and cpufreq just does not work
on them anymore. It's a good thing, as they clock down further, run
cooler at idle, draw less power and respond quicker, and has the
ability to internally overclock single threads if the others are idle.
All in all, much preferable. I just needed to switch off tickless
kernel for a specific version, as I think there is a bug in that
kernel version on mobile i7's, as there is no such problems on my
desktop i7.
